# Privacy Policy for GTOD **Last updated:** February 28, 2026 GTOD ("we," "our," or "the app") is a fitness and activity tracking app. This policy describes what data we collect and how we use it. ## Data We Collect ### Account & Profile - **Email address** – Used for sign-in and account recovery - **Password** – Stored securely (hashed) for authentication - **Display name** – Shown to other users in groups and social features - **Profile photo** – Optional; used for your profile and in activity feeds ### Activity Data - **Activities** – Type, duration, distance, calories, and other metrics you log - **GPS data** – Location points during runs, rides, and hikes (when you record with GPS enabled) - **Photos** – Images you attach to activities ### Device & Usage - **Push notification token** – To send you notifications (e.g., group updates, daily reminders) - **Device info** – Used for compatibility and support ### Third-Party Integrations - **Garmin Connect** – If you connect Garmin, we sync activities and fitness data (e.g., VO2 max) from your Garmin account. We do not store your Garmin password; authentication is handled via secure OAuth. ## How We Use Your Data - **Provide the app** – Store and display your activities, groups, and profile - **Social features** – Show your activities to friends and group members (based on your settings) - **Notifications** – Send push notifications you’ve opted into (e.g., group announcements, daily Guess The Word reminder) - **Improve the app** – Fix bugs and improve performance ## Data Storage & Security - Data is stored on Supabase (cloud infrastructure) - Passwords are hashed; we never see your plain-text password - We use industry-standard security practices ## Sharing Your Data - **Other users** – Your profile (name, photo) and activities may be visible to friends and group members, depending on your settings - **Service providers** – We use Supabase for hosting and Expo for push notifications - **Legal** – We may disclose data if required by law or to protect rights and safety We do not sell your personal data. ## Your Choices - **Location** – You can deny location permission; you can still log activities manually - **Photos** – You can skip adding photos to your profile or activities - **Notifications** – You can disable push notifications in your device settings or in-app preferences (e.g., Guess The Word daily reminder) - **Garmin** – You can disconnect Garmin at any time from your profile ## Data Retention - We retain your data while your account is active - After account deletion, we remove your data within a reasonable period ## Children GTOD is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. ## Changes We may update this policy. We will notify you of material changes via the app or email. ## Contact For questions about this policy or your data, contact: raceacedev@gmail.com
